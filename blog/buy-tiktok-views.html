Which is Better on TikTok? Likes or Views

Both metrics are important, but they serve different purposes:

Views indicate reach and visibility.

Likes show engagement and content appreciation.

To grow your account, focus on increasing both, but prioritize views for initial growth and likes for sustained engagement.

How TikTok Works

TikTok operates on a unique algorithm that:

  • Analyzes watch time, likes, shares, and comments.
  • Promotes videos with higher engagement metrics.
  • Personalizes the For You page (FYP) based on user behavior.

What Are Likes on TikTok?

Likes are a form of engagement showing users’ appreciation for your content. High likes:

  • Improve your video’s algorithmic ranking.
  • Encourage more users to interact with your profile.

What Are Shares on TikTok?

Shares occur when a user forwards your video to others. Shares:

  • Increase your content’s reach.
  • Signal to TikTok’s algorithm that your video is valuable.

What Parameter Should You Focus on the Most in TikTok?

Focus on:

  • Views for initial traction.
  • Engagement Rate (likes, comments, shares) for sustained growth.
  • Retention Rate (how long users watch your video) to rank higher on the algorithm.
